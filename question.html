  • 贵校的就业服务中心可以为中国学生提供哪些服务？

    LaunchPad is the team which is dedicated to helping you feel clear about your career. The support is available to all of our Chinese students, when you are studying at York St John University and after you leave. LaunchPad offers everything you need to kick-start your career. From employer events, to LinkedIn and resume sessions, advice on further study, volunteering, work experience opportunities, placements, year in industry and study abroad options. The team understand how the workplace is changing and creates initiatives to meet your needs, like our Grad2Director programme which offers online development, expert one-to-one coaching and business start-up funding so that you can launch your own business, as many of our recent graduates have.

    LaunchPad团队致力于帮助你明确自己的职业规划。我们所有的中国学生，无论在约克圣约翰大学学习期间，还是在毕业后，都可以得到我们的支持。LaunchPad为你提供职业生涯开始所需要的一切。包括招聘会，领英，简历，继续深造顾问，志愿服务，工作机会，实习和留学择校建议。我们的团队了解工作环境的变化，并根据您的需求制定计划。比如我们有一个Grad2Director项目，该项目提供在线支持、专家一对一指导和创业资金，这样您就可以像我们的许多应届毕业生一样，开创自己的事业。
